West Pixel Games

Welcome to West Pixel Games! We are a small indie game development company who specializes in small shooting games. About our team: We are a small 1 man team with high goals and aspirations. We are currently working on one 3d space game and one 2d game that is currently in development. We use both Unity, Blender and Gimp to help design and make our games. We design games that are simple and easy to play. These games have smooth controls and easy to control.
