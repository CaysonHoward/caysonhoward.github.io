Purpose

The purpose of the site is to inform the people who play our games and would like to learn more about who we are. This webisite will be used a medium for comunicating with our players and inform them of the games we have as well as future releases.

The people who will visit this site are people who are interested in the games we make. Other people who might visit will be interested in games as well as future releases from our company. The website should be accessable from any form of internet connected device for the best viewing pleasure of potential customers.

Home

Welcome to West Pixel Games! We are a small indie game development company who specializes in small shooting games. About our team: We are a small 1 man team with high goals and aspirations. We are currently working on one 3d space game and one 2d game that is currently in development. We use both Unity, Blender and Gimp to help design and make our games. We design games that are simple and easy to play. These games have smooth controls and easy to control.

[Page 2]

Unity: Unity is a game engine that we use to design our games. It is simple and easy to use. We use C# to code our games and program the back end. Gimp: Gimp is a drawing app similar to Adobe photoshop. We use this to draw prototype images for our games as well as concept art. We also use this for out game design for smaller 2d projects Blender: Blender is a 3d object designer app. We use this to design simple 3d objects for our more complex games. This is a more complex program that is rather difficult to use.
